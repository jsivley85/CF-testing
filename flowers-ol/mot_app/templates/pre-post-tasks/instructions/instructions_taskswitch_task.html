Instructions

  • The goal of this experiment is to measure the ability to switch your attention.
  • In each trial, the instruction word "Color" or "Shape" will be shown to you.
  • Depending on the instruction word, your task changes.
  • When you see the word "Color," you have to answer whether the following object is "blue" or "red."
    • For the "Color" trial, please press "f" or "j" for the red or blue, respectively, as soon as possible.
  • In contrast, when you see the word "Shape," you have to answer whether the following object is "square" or "circle."
    • For the "Shape" trial, please press "f" or "j" for the square or circle, respectively, as soon as possible.
  • You can check the practice trials of this task from the following window.
