Instructions

  • The goal of this experiment is to measure the attention ability of multiple targets.
  • In each trial, you will see a brief presentation of a cross target with four stripe objects.
  • You have to answer two tasks for each trial.
  • First, you have to answer which of the lines are longer using your keyboard.
    • If the vertical line is longer, then please press "F" key.
    • If the horizontal line is longer, then please press "J" key.
  • After this response, you have to answer which of the four objects had the strong contrast stripe by clicking one of four buttons.
    • The "strong contrast" means that white and black colors are more differentiated.
  • Please keep in mind that you have to answer at least the first question correctly. If not, your response for the second question will not be recorded.
  • You can check the practice trials of this task from the following window.
